Chocolate peanut butter iced coffee

Description

Super easy iced coffee that can be made with items you most likely have.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup soy milk
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 packet granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as splenda)

Instructions

  1. Pour coffee into as ice cube tray; freeze until coffee ice cubes form, 1 to hours
  2. Combine coffee ice cubes, soy milk, chocolate syrup, peanut butter, vanilla extract and no-calorie sweetener in a blender. Cover and blend until frothy.

Nutrition facts (per serving)

284 Calories

11g Fat

37g Carbs

9g Protein
